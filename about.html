About Us

  • The beginning

    Our company chunky studios began on the 28th of September 1984 as a group who focuses on the entertainment industry. Our founder Marcos Chico Ornandes  had a vision on which will keep the company striving for Decades to come.

  • The Vision

    The company's main goal is consistency and improvement. Our Animations or in-game animations are used to develop a deeper intensity to the viewer, providing the best possible experience to our audience.

  • Marcos Chico Ornandes

    Marcos Chico Ornandes or Marcho for short, is our company founder born in January 30th 1899. He come from a family of 2 parents and 37 Siblings, him being the youngest. His inspiration came from his family and there love of the wilderness, so he began to explore how to recreate the experience and scenery into animations.
